About

Tight Carbon Dioxide

Our project is a design based on IoT system to monitor climate change bad impacts. Our website is the destination of the data collected by the sensors. Our data are air quality, temperature, humidity, and soil moisture. This website analyzes and integrates the data and show the relations between the variables in graphs, charts and excel cheats. The purpose of this site is the prediction of the impacts of climate change, CO2 emissions in specification.
